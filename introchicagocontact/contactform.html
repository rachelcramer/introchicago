Intro Chicago welcomes Chef Collante

Chef Bryan Collante was named Chef of the Year at the 2017 Jean Banchet Awards for his work at this West Loop spot from the owners of Alinea and Next. Now Chef Collante is bringing his talent to Intro Chicago! To celebrate, we are offering his current Thai-inspired tasting menu featuring six courses including seared Kobe beef and Fried Prawn Cake with Red Curry for the whole month of April.

Tickets are $85 Sunday through Thursday and $95 Friday and Saturday, with a beverage package available for another $35.
